Consulation
Our service provides the eye of local art professional Stephen Molyneux, who's worked in galleries all over London since the early 2000's, hanging some of the great names of the 20th Century.

We'll arrange a visit to your space and the consultation will go over what your aspirations are for your collection.

Needless to say there are a myriad of decisions when it comes to hanging your beloved artworks and we're here to either help formulate a plan or realise ideas you already have in mind.

Once we've formed a clear vision as to how the work will be situated, we'll install your pieces to museum grade standard.
